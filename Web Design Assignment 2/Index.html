Profile

Eliana Barela is an artist from Tom√©, New Mexico. She specializes in acrylic and oil paintings, as well as photography and woodworking. Her Hispanic culture, family, and faith drive her artistry. She is majoring in Art Education and plans to get her master's in Art Therapy. For the past year, she has worked as a children's painting teacher at a local art center and is also pursuing her dream of becoming a Santera. A Santera is an artist who creates Santos y Revultos and other Spanish-style religious artwork all through natural materials found here in New Mexico!
